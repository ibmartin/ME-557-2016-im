Controls:

The paddle on your side moves with the cursor on screen.  Match it to the position of the ball to hit back.  If the paddle is moving when the ball is hit, some of its momentum will be added to the ball.

Mouse:
Right Click: Trackball Rotation

Keys:
1. Enter Default Camera mode
2. Enter Spectator camera mode
3. Enter chase camera mode
Spacebar. Reset the ball
Esc. Exit the application

When either player scores, a small indicator with a light should appear on the side of the player who scored a point.
